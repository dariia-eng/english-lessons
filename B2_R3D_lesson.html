🥪 The Feedback Sandwich

Before you start — learn this technique. You'll use it in the roleplay below.

🍞 TOP SLICE
Start positive
▼
🥩 FILLING
The difficult part
▼
🍞 BOTTOM SLICE
End with a forward step
🟢 Positive opener
  • "The first thing to say is that customers really love your…"
  • "One of your real strengths is…"
  • "Everyone appreciates how…"
🔴 Honest concern
  • "However, some people have mentioned that…"
  • "Unfortunately, that was the biggest complaint."
  • "Some people felt you could be a bit more…"
🟡 Forward step
  • "Overall, you're doing a great job."
  • "You might like to try being a bit more…"
  • "Perhaps you could bear that in mind."
💡 Why use it? It keeps the conversation respectful and motivating. The person hears the criticism but doesn't feel attacked.

📋 Useful phrases — click to expand
👍 Positive feedback
  • The first thing to say is that [customers are very positive].
  • They really appreciate [your punctuality].
  • [Cleanliness] is one of your strong points.
  • Overall, people feel that [you do your job very well].
⚠️ Negative feedback
  • Unfortunately, many people felt that […].
  • That was the biggest complaint.
  • Some people felt you could be a bit more [professional].
  • The general feeling was that [the service is a bit too informal].
💡 Suggesting improvements
  • You might like to try being a bit more [organised].
  • Perhaps you could [bear that in mind].
  • It's something that could be improved.
✅ Responding to feedback
  • I'll take that on board.
  • From my point of view, [I think it's my job to…].
  • I want to explain my side.
  • I understand. I'll work on that.
