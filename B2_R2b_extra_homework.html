📐 Quick Reminder — Passive Voice

Form: subject + to be (tense) + past participle [+ by + agent]

  • Past simple: My complaint was ignored.
  • Present perfect: No refund has been issued.
  • Modal: This should be addressed immediately.
  • Past perfect: The booking had been cancelled before we arrived.

Rewrite in the Passive

Rewrite each sentence using the passive voice. Keep the same tense. Include by + agent only where it adds useful information.
🎬 Dune: Part Two / Gladiator II / Wicked
1. The studio hasn't announced the sequel to Dune: Part Two yet. (present perfect)
→ The sequel to Dune: Part Two
2. Critics strongly criticised the ending of Gladiator II. (past simple)
→ The ending of Gladiator II
3. Nobody had warned us about the noise before the film started. (past perfect)
→ We
4. The cinema is currently investigating several complaints. (present continuous)
→ Several complaints
5. They will release the director's cut of Wicked next year. (future with will)
→ The director's cut of Wicked
6. The management should have dealt with this issue before the premiere. (modal perfect passive)
→ This issue
